Day 16 - Making Sense

Apr 02, 2020 covid-19 personal

Something else I didn’t know about this quarantine is how little sense I’d be able to make of it all. On a daily basis, I oscillate between feeling like it’s the end of the world and everything is normal.

Like, within a one hour span, I’m refinancing my mortgage and reading an article about the coast guard telling cruise ships to turn away from American ports.
